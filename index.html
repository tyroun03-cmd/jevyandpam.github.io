Quick Answers

Everything you need to know

Can I bring a plus one?

Due to limited venue capacity, we kindly ask that you only bring guests who are specifically named on your invitation. If you received a "& Guest" on your invitation, then yes! Please indicate their name when you RSVP. For any questions, feel free to reach out to us directly.

Are toddlers & infants welcome?

We truly adore your little ones, however, our wedding will be a child-free celebration. We hope this allows you to relax and fully enjoy the evening with us. We sincerely appreciate your understanding and hope you can take this opportunity to enjoy a special night off.

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive at least 30 minutes before the ceremony starts at 11:00 AM. This will give you ample time to find parking, settle in, sign the guest book, and be comfortably seated before the processional begins. Latecomers may be asked to wait until after the ceremony to enter.

Is there parking available?

Yes! Complimentary parking is available at Ricky & Weng's Garden Resort. Our parking attendants will be on site to guide you to the designated parking area upon arrival. Carpooling is encouraged as spaces are limited.

How do I RSVP?

You can RSVP using the form below! Simply fill in your name, email address, attendance status, and number of guests. Please let us know your response by May 30, 2026 so we can finalize arrangements with our vendors. We can't wait to celebrate with you!
